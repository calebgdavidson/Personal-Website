About Haworth First Assembly

Haworth First is an

Assemblies of God

church made
up of people from
many different backgrounds
coming together
to worship Jesus Christ.

Pastor Mark Davidson was born in Colorado in 1969 to Gary and Wilma Davidson who returned to Oklahoma in 1970 and became the pastors of Haworth First Assembly until 1980 when they moved to Dublin Ireland as missionaries.

Mark devoted his life to full time ministry at 17 and graduated from Central Bible College in Springfield, MO in 1995 and bacame an ordained Assemblies of God minister in 1997 and stepped into the role of Youth Pastor in Oklahoma. In 2005 he took over as Lead Pastor of The Bridge Church in Bristol, TN where he pastored for 7 years.

In the spring of 2015 Haworth First Assembly of God was looking for a new Lead Pastor. Pastor Mark received a text from his mother about the position when he read the text he felt "a peace and destiny" and then responded with a big "Yes!!!" He knew in that moment that he was saying yes to God's will.

In the days ahead he trusted God's plan of bringing his life full circle to the church he first felt the calling of God to be a pastor as a child. On July 5th 2015 Pastor Mark and Julie Davidson became the new Lead Pastors.

Our Lead Pastors

Mark and Julie have 5 children and 3 grandchildren; Caleb and his wife Brooke and their son Ashton and daughter Kit, Faith and her husband Charlie and their daughter Charity, Jacob, Leila, and John.

Mark loves to spending time in the word, hunting, fishing, riding horses, and reading books from his favorite authors Paul Tripp, Mark Buchanan, Robert Morris, and Eugene Peterson.

Julie is very involved in the community, loves to travel, read, and study God's word. She is also the Haworth Public Schools Elementary School Librarian.
